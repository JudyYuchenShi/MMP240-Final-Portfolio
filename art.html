Sculpture is an expression of art. I was lucky enough to study sculpture, and the first work was called the moon. My definition is that a changing moon can take any form. This also reflects my thinking on the philosophy of life. Everything in life is changing, and I can only cope with all the changes if I am fully prepared. At the same time, I also like to observe the small details of life, such as how to make a mushroom. The devil is in the detail.
