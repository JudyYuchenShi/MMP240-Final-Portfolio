Package design is an important link to present the characteristics of commodities. I designed a package design of a travel series for jcoco. The theme is three cities, New York, three times and Paris. I want to remind people of the city they used to live in or visit when they eat chocolate with the flavor of the city. Because each chocolate taste is different, I use the sharp contrast of colors to show the different characteristics of each city.
